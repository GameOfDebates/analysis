Game of Debates

Meera Parat, Vishal Disawar, Pranav Boopathy, Haocheng Zhang, Emily Chao

The goal of our visualization is to show the overall picture of the Republican and Democratic party primary debates.

How much talking time does each candidate get to talk during a debate? What kinds of topics are discussed during these debates? Does their talking time influence the emotions conveyed in their speech during debates?

Transcripts courtesy of the Washington Post, New York Times, CNN, CBS News, and Time.

Talk-time data courtesy of Politico, InsideGov, NPR, CNN.
